About the profession
of Songwriter

A songwriter is a songwriter. He or she comes up with an idea, writes a melody – from scratch to a finished composition. A songwriter can be either the performer or a separate person who writes but does not perform the music.

To become a songwriter, you don't need an education or perfect pitch. And you can train your sense of rhythm and harmony. You'll start practicing on the course: learn how to write songs and music in Ableton Live, record several melodies and tracks. Learn how the music market works, understand how to work with artists and labels. As a result, you'll be able to promote and monetize your work.

What does the profession of a songwriter give?

Opportunity to work with artists

Many musicians use songwriters, and big stars create hits with a whole team of authors. You will have a chance to hear your songs on the big stage and earn good money from royalties and royalties.

Creative realization

If you have long wanted to express your ideas in creativity, but did not know how to do it, songwriting will help you open up. You will be able to turn a stream of thoughts into a beautiful story and implement an idea from your head. It is only important to master the basics and practice constantly.

Possibility to work remotely

Songwriters almost never have a clear schedule, much less an office. Some find it convenient to write music at home, while others go to the studio to work with the equipment and discuss the track with the artist and producer. It all depends on you.
